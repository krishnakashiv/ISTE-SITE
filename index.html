From the Principal's Desk

It gives me immense pleasure to see that our college has initiated steps to actively involve in the Indian Society For Technical  Education.
RAIT has climbed several notches since its inception mainly because of one goal for improving and updating our technical know- how. Being an active participant in ISTE will help us to gain insightful experiences as well as expertise in our domain due to our interface with the best of resources and resource persons that ISTE being with it.
Societies like ISTE does the task of sponsoring projects, holding national & state level competitions conducting of workshops, holding project exhibitions and competitions. this proves to be an excellent platform for both teaches and students to test and evaluate themselves at the national level and also helps to enhance their exposure to technology and resources. I wish all the members and participants the very best.
